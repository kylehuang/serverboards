# 2. Usage tracking

Serverboards uses an internal service to track the use of the application so that
we get information on the current use, errors and future areas of improvements.

This information is of huge value for us, but you can disable it at your user
preferences.

This information is accesible by Serverboards SL.

This information is never shared with third parties, and only includes
information of location of errors and navigational information. It never
includes personal information.
